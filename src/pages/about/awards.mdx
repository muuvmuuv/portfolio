## Photography Award "Blende 2013"

For 45 years now, Prophoto GmbH, together with daily newspapers and other cooperation
partners, has been awarding the nationwide photo competition Aperture. The competition,
which runs in two stages, offers amateur and hobby photographers the opportunity to prove
their skills every year.

> https://www.prophoto-online.de/blende-fotowettbewerb.html
